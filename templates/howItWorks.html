How it Works

Write down what you accomplished today! And everyday, from now on.

Why

Do you remember what you did the day exactly a week ago? How about the day before that?

The idea behind LifeTracker is to try to make every day memerable.

How

Every day you write down the single most important thing that happened that day.

Be it you explored a new part of town, made a new friend, or finished your weight loss goal.
